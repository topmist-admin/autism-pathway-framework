## Limitations

### Scientific Limitations

1. **Hypothesis-generating only**: All outputs are preliminary hypotheses requiring independent experimental validation
2. **Pathway definitions are incomplete**: Curated pathways are biased toward well-studied biology and do not capture all biological processes
3. **Static models**: The framework does not account for:
   - Developmental timing
   - Cell-type specificity
   - Compensatory mechanisms
   - Environmental influences
   - Gene-environment interactions

4. **No causal claims**: Associations identified do not establish causality
5. **Cohort-dependent**: Results may not generalize across populations or sequencing technologies

### Technical Limitations

1. **Data quality dependent**: Outputs are only as reliable as input data
2. **Parameter sensitivity**: Results may vary with analytical choices
3. **Computational approximations**: Network propagation and clustering involve simplifying assumptions
